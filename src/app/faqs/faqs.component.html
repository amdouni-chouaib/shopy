Frequently Asked Questions

What if the item received is defective ?

In case of defect of the article, you simply send us by email the photos. In less than 24 hours, a delivery person will pick up the item from your home to return it to the seller and you will be reimbursed for all expenses incurred.

What payment methods are accepted ?

You can pay in Cash, by Credit Card, by E-dinar Card or via the Runpay terminals available everywhere in Tunisia.

Should I go to the Post Office to sell or buy ?

No, a courier will come to your door to pick up or deliver an item to you. The delivery and the collection of article is done on all the Tunisian territory without exception.

Is my private information protected ?

Your private information (telephone number, address, age, account number, identity card number, etc.) is carefully kept in our databases and is not disclosed or sold to any third party, or even to other users.
